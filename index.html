About Nursing Connect Africa

Empowering Nurses.
Strengthening Healthcare.
Transforming Communities.

Nursing Connect Africa (NCA) is a pan-African Non-Profit Company (NPC) committed to uniting, empowering, and advancing nurses across Africa. We provide professional development, advocacy, mutual support, leadership opportunities, and community health initiatives that strengthen both the nursing profession and the communities it serves.

Why We Exist

Nurses dedicate their lives to caring for others, yet many face professional, financial, and personal challenges without adequate support. Nursing Connect Africa was founded to ensure that no nurse walks that journey alone. We are building a united network where every nurse can grow, lead, and make a lasting impact.

Our Programmes

Supporting nurses through every stage of their professional journey.

Nursing Connect Africa delivers its impact through a suite of integrated programmes designed to address the professional, financial, social, and emotional needs of nurses.

Flagship Programme

Healing Hand Network

NCA's dedicated mutual support programme — designed for all nurses, young and old, from every category. By wearing the same uniform, we create unity and visibility in our communities.

⚰️

Funeral Cover

Subsidised funeral cover for NCA members and their immediate family members. No nurse should face the burden of loss without support from their professional community.

🏥

Disability & Illness Support

Financial protection and support for nurses in case of injury, illness, or disability. NCA ensures members are covered when they can no longer work.

📚

Education & Training

Resources, bursaries, and scholarships for nurses to continue learning and growing professionally. Future goal: fully funded educational programmes for career advancement.

🎉

Seminars & Social Events

Regular gatherings to teach, entertain, network, and allow nurses to de-stress. Opportunities for mentoring, knowledge-sharing, and building lasting professional relationships.

👔

Community Visibility & Unity

A standardised NCA uniform for all members representing solidarity, strength, and professional pride. When our communities see nurses standing together, they see healthcare that cares.

💼

Business & Private Practice Development

Equipping nurses with business acumen, financial literacy, and practical tools to start and sustain their own private practices. From business planning to marketing and compliance.

🏆

Annual Nursing Excellence Awards

An annual awards ceremony celebrating nurses making outstanding impact. Categories include Community Health Champion, Nurse Entrepreneur of the Year, Mentor of the Year, and Lifetime Achievement.

Governance Structure

Democratic leadership and accountable governance

NCA operates through elected leadership structures guided by a constitution and strong ethical oversight.

Executive Board

Provides strategic direction, policy approval, financial oversight, and organisational accountability.

Country Chapters

Deliver local implementation of NCA programmes, recruitment, events, and advocacy.

Advisory Council

Offers expert guidance on clinical standards, governance, legal compliance, and strategic partnerships.

Secretariat

Coordinates day-to-day administration, finance, communications, member services, and programme reporting.

Training Arm

Generates sustainable revenue through CPD training, workshops, and corporate wellness services.

Strategic 12-Month Rollout Plan

A Practical Roadmap for Launch and Growth

The first year focuses on establishing a solid foundation, launching core programmes, building membership, and demonstrating impact.

Q1

Months 1–3

✓ Complete NPC registration and legal compliance
✓ Establish founding committee and interim Executive Board
✓ Launch digital platforms (website, social media, WhatsApp groups)
✓ Design and produce NCA branded uniform
✓ Recruit founding members through direct outreach and social media campaigns
Q2

Months 4–6

✓ Launch first training programmes and CPD workshops
✓ Introduce paid membership tiers and payment processing
✓ Establish pilot country chapters in key provinces
✓ Begin corporate and NGO partnership development
✓ Launch Healing Hand Network funeral and disability cover
Q3

Months 7–9

✓ Host first regional virtual nursing summit
✓ Expand job and locum opportunity board
✓ Implement community health outreach programmes
✓ Grow membership base through referral incentives and campus drives
✓ Begin mentorship programme pairing senior and junior nurses
Q4

Months 10–12

✓ Host inaugural annual Nursing Connect Africa Summit
✓ Secure long-term sponsors and strategic partners
✓ Publish first annual impact report
✓ Evaluate programme outcomes and membership satisfaction
✓ Plan Year 2 expansion including additional country chapters

Improved nurse competence and confidence through accessible training

💼

Increased employment and income-generating opportunities for members

🤝

Stronger professional nurse identity and solidarity across the network

🏥

Improved access to quality healthcare in underserved communities

📊

Sustainable nurse-led healthcare initiatives with measurable outcomes
