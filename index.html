About Us

Built by Nurses, for Nurses

We understand the unique challenges nurses face — because we are nurses. Our network exists to ensure no nurse walks alone.

OUR STORY

How Healing Hand Network Began

Founded in July 2025 by a dedicated group of nurses, Healing Hand Network was created to address the unique challenges nurses face every day. Our goal is to ensure no nurse suffers alone — whether from illness, financial hardship, stress, or lack of support.

We welcome all nurses, regardless of category or age, to join us in creating a stronger, united community. By wearing the same uniform, we create visibility and solidarity in our communities — showing that nurses stand together.

Our Vision

A united nursing community where every nurse is supported, empowered, and visible — working together to build a stronger future for those who dedicate their lives to caring for others.

Our Mission

We are committed to making a real difference through:

  • Subsidised funeral cover, disability support, and protection for nurses and their immediate families
  • Unity and visibility through the same uniform for all members
  • Emotional debriefing, wellness, and peer support programmes
  • Seminars, workshops, and professional development opportunities
Programs & Benefits

What We Offer Our Members

Comprehensive support for nurses at every stage of their career and life journey.

Funeral Cover

Support for nurses and their immediate families during times of loss. No one should face the burden of funeral costs alone.

Disability & Illness Support

Financial and emotional protection for nurses in case of injury or illness, ensuring security when you need it most.

Education & Training

Resources and scholarships for nurses to continue learning and growing professionally.

Future Goal

Seminars & Social Events

Regular workshops, debriefing sessions, and social gatherings to connect nurses and build community bonds.

Community Visibility & Unity

Shared uniforms and public presence campaigns to make nurses visible and respected in every community.

Networking & Mentoring

Connecting nurses across categories and experience levels to share knowledge, guidance, and career support.

Our Future Goals

  • Expanded retirement support packages for nurses
  • Fully funded educational programs and professional development bursaries
  • National visibility campaigns for nursing communities
